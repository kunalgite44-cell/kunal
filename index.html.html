Virat Kohli

Born: 5 November 1988

Country: India

Role: Batsman

Batting: Right-handed

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and one of the most successful batsmen in modern cricket. He has represented India in all formats and is known for his aggressive playing style and consistency.

Early Life

Virat Kohli was born in Delhi, India. He showed interest in cricket from a young age and trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy.

International Career

Kohli made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2008. Over the years, he became a key player and later served as the captain of the national team.

Achievements

  • Former captain of India
  • Multiple international centuries
  • Recipient of major sports awards
